Bobby Fischer was an American chess player widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. He became the youngest player to win the US Championship at age 14 and famously defeated Boris Spassky in the 1972 World Chess Championship.
Garry Kasparov is a retired Russian chess grandmaster and former World Chess Champion. He is widely considered one of the greatest chess players of all time and has been an outspoken political activist in recent years.
Anatoly Karpov is a retired Russian chess grandmaster and former World Chess Champion. He held the world No. 1 ranking for a total of 102 months and was known for his positional style of play.
